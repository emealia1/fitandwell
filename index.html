Nutrition & Diet

While it's true that nutrition and diet is evolving, there are some nutrition basics you can keep in mind. By knowing these basics, you'll be better equipped to continue and thrive.

Healthy menus and shopping

  • Your shopping strategies should focus on fresh and unprocessed foods. That means spending more time in the produce section, where you can stock up on fruits and vegetables.
  • Keep an eye out too for whole grains and low-fat dairy products.

Nutritional supplements

  • Nutritional supplements are meant to complement a healthy diet, not replace it.
  • If you're generally healthy and eat a wide variety of foods, you probably don't need nutritional supplements. In certain cases, however, fortified foods and dietary supplements may be useful in providing one or more nutrients.

Exercise & a Healthy Lifestyle

Exercise is one of the easiest and most effective ways of improving both your physical and mental health. By focusing on activities you enjoy and tailoring a regular exercise routine to your needs, you can experience health benefits and improve your own life.

Types of Exercise

  • Aerobic activities like running, cycling, and swimming strengthen your heart and increase your endurance.
  • Strength training like weight lifting or resistance training builds muscle and bone mass, improves balance and prevents falls. It’s one of the best counters to frailty in old age.

Easy Exercise Tips

  • Move more in your daily life. Look at your daily routine and consider ways to include activity here and there. Even small activities can add up over a day.
  • Focus on activities you enjoy. You’ll find it easier to stick with an exercise program that’s built around those activities.

Wellness & Stress Management

Stress is a normal psychological and physical reaction to the ever increasing demands of life. Stress management gives you a range of tools to reset your internal alarm system. Start practicing a range of stress management techniques today.

Stress Relief

  • The first step in successful stress relief is deciding to make a change in how you manage stress. The next step is identifying your stress triggers.
  • Once you've identified your stress triggers, you can start thinking about strategies for dealing with them. Sometimes the solution may be as easy as turning off the TV when the evening news is too distressing.

Relaxation Techniques

  • Relaxation is invaluable for maintaining your health and well-being, and repairing the toll that stress takes on your mind and body.
  • Common relaxation techniques include meditation, mindfulness, tai chi and yoga. For a more active approach, walking outdoors or participating in a sporting sports activity can be relaxing.
